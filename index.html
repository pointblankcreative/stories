Speak Up For Alberta’s public health care

We believe in people over profits. That’s why Albertans across the province are standing together to protect the public health care system.

The last two years of the pandemic have been long and incredibly straining strain on our health care system. And health care workers showed up for us every single day nevertheless. Now Jason Kenney and the UCP want to privatize our health care, prioritizing profits over patients and health care workers.

We’re not going to let them.

Share your story of how the public health care system impacted your life. Your stories will show how important it is for Albertans to have strong, quality public health care.
